About Tamil Nadu Hospital

தமிழ்நாடு மருத்துவமனை பற்றி

Established in 1981, Tamil Nadu Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare innovation, providing exceptional medical services to our community.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology, and our team of dedicated professionals is committed to delivering personalized care.
